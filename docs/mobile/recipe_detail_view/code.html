Preparation

01
INITIAL_PHASE

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Lightly grease a medium-sized baking dish with olive oil or parchment paper to prevent adhesion during the curing process.

02
INTEGRATION

In a large architectural-grade mixing bowl, combine the spinach, eggs, and Greek yogurt. Fold until the structural integrity of the mixture is uniform and consistent.

03
THERMAL_PROCESSING

Transfer the compound to the prepared dish. Bake for approximately 15-18 minutes until the edges exhibit a golden-brown finish and the center reaches a solidified state.
